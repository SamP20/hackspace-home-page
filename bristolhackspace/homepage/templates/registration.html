Registration

If you’d like to find out more or you’re interested in joining the Hackspace, then please drop in to one of our open / social evenings.

These run most Thursdays from 7pm until late, and you’ll be able to take a look around out facilities and meet a few members. No booking is necessary, just turn up!

When you visit, you’ll also be able to get everything you need to join the Hackspace.
